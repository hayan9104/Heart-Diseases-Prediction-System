Understanding Heart Arrhythmia

Your comprehensive guide to heart rhythm disorders and solutions

What is Heart Arrhythmia?

A heart arrhythmia is an irregular heartbeat where electrical signals that coordinate heart rhythms don't work properly. This can cause your heart to beat too fast, too slow, or irregularly.

Common Symptoms

Primary Symptoms

  • Fluttering in chest
  • Racing heartbeat
  • Slow heartbeat
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath

Additional Signs

  • Anxiety
  • Fatigue
  • Lightheadedness
  • Sweating
  • Fainting

⚠️ When to Seek Emergency Care

Call 911 immediately if you experience:

  • Severe chest pain
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Fainting or severe dizziness

Solutions & Treatment Options

Medical Treatments

  • Medications
  • Cardioversion
  • Catheter ablation
  • Pacemaker implantation

Lifestyle Changes

  • Reduce stress
  • Regular exercise
  • Healthy diet
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol
  • Quit smoking

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain healthy blood pressure
  • Get adequate sleep
  • Regular check-ups
  • Monitor heart rhythm
